STOCKS AND SHARE MARKET

Stocks and the share market are two terms that are often used interchangeably, but they actually refer to different aspects of the same thing. The share market is a place where stocks are bought and sold, and stocks are ownership units in a publicly-traded company. In this blog, we will discuss the basics of stocks and the share market, including how they work, why people invest in them, and some important factors to consider when investing

People invest in stocks for various reasons, but the primary goal is usually to earn a return on their investment. Stocks have the potential to provide high returns over the long term, as companies can grow and increase their profits over time. Additionally, stocks can provide a source of passive income through dividends, which are payments made to shareholders from a company's profits.
